The one hobby I can't get enough of


Reading for pleasure engages the imagination and it is an effective way to manage stress (until the characters are the ones stressing you out) by increasing enjoyment and relaxation. Recreational reading also can have benefits for your concentration, vocabulary, reading and writing skills, and improving working memory. Leisure reading has been shown to increase an individual's communication skills, understanding of themselves and others who are different from them, and develop overall empathy skills. Overall it is just fun diving into different world's. Readers select from a wide range of extended texts, including but not exclusive to narrative fiction, nonfiction, picture books, e-books, comic books, and graphic novels.
